Kid Zone Idaho Falls: The Indoor Playground Your Kids Will Love

If you're looking for a place to burn off some energy rain or shine, Kid Zone is worth adding to your list.

Family Idaho Falls Things to Do

If you're looking for a place to burn off some energy rain or shine, Kid Zone in Idaho Falls is worth adding to your list. Judging by the packed house even on a random Wednesday afternoon, plenty of local families already have.

Kid Zone is an indoor playground designed for kids ages 0 to 12, with huge play structures, a dedicated toddler area, and age-appropriate zones that keep things fun and safe for a wide range of ages. The facility prides itself on daily sanitizing, soft play equipment, and attentive staff, so parents can actually relax while the kids run wild.

Celebrating Something Special?

Kid Zone is a go-to spot for birthday parties. They offer four private party rooms with setup and cleanup handled by staff, so you can show up and enjoy the moment. Outside food is welcome, making it easy to bring a custom cake or your kids' favorite treats.

You can also book the entire facility for private events — ideal for larger groups or exclusive gatherings.

Plan Your Visit

Kid Zone Idaho Falls
📍 Address 406 S Woodruff Ave, Idaho Falls, ID
🕐 Hours Sun–Thu 9am–6pm  ·  Fri–Sat 9am–8pm
🧦 Note Grip socks are required for play
👶 Ages 0–12 years old
🌐 Website kidzoneut.com

Memberships, play passes, and birthday party bookings are all available on their website. Kid Zone also has a second location in Clearfield, Utah, for families traveling through or with ties to the area.
