3. Registered agent
What is a registered agent? Idaho law requires every LLC to have a registered agent — a person or company with a physical Idaho address available during business hours to receive legal documents, tax notices, and state correspondence on your behalf. Your registered agent's name and address become public record on the Idaho Secretary of State website. If you designate yourself, your personal name and home address will be publicly visible. Using a registered agent service keeps your personal information off public records.
