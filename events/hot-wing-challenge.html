The Challenge
What Is The Hot Wing Challenge?

Inspired by the legendary YouTube show Hot Ones, participants eat 10 chicken wings — each coated in a progressively hotter hot sauce from the official Hot Ones Season 29 lineup. It starts easy. It doesn't stay that way.

From the creamy garlic warmth of Wing #1 to the face-melting fury of The Last Dab: Thermageddon on Wing #10, this is a test of willpower, taste buds, and sheer determination. Finish all 10 wings, and you walk away a champion. Tap out, and you'll have a great story anyway.

Know Before You Go
Challenge Rules
  • Each participant eats 10 chicken wings, one at a time, in order from mildest to hottest.
  • Each wing is coated in the corresponding sauce from the Hot Ones Season 29 lineup.
  • You must finish each wing before moving on to the next. No skipping sauces.
  • You may drink water or milk between wings. No other beverages or food allowed during the challenge.
  • If you tap out at any point, you're done — no re-entry. No shame. These sauces are no joke.
  • Participants who complete all 10 wings will be declared official East Idaho Hot Wing Challenge Champions, featured on our social media channels, and receive a professionally produced video about their business courtesy of East Idaho Media.
  • All participants must sign a liability waiver before competing. See disclaimers below.
  • The event will be filmed. By participating, you consent to being photographed and recorded for East Idaho Media content.
